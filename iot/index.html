Business Thesis

Using a clear, repeatable framework, small, in-house teams can develop and sell simple hardware + SaaS products that improve efficiency and grow revenue in businesses that have been slow to adopt new technology.

Framework

Decrease Risk!

both market risk and execution risk

Formalize failure in business model

Provide means for process introspection and improvement

Decrease day-to-day cognitive overhead

Product failure has a much lower consequence on success of company Embracing product failure as a regular part of the business allows for faster iteration through product ideas and continuous improvement of the product development process.
Acme will not be based around only one product. It will continuously research, prototype, develop, and sell products that can help small and medium-sized businesses. Each product will follow the same process from conceptualization, to development, to growing and maintaining the business. Throughout this process the product will be continually tested to determine whether it can be a profitable business. A product may be shelved at any time if it fails this test. When a product is shelved the team moves on to another product.

Team

Autonomy, Mastery, Purpose4

  1. Give teams the freedom and the support to do what they think is right
  2. Compose the teams of highly skilled individuals who are also motivated to grow
  3. Give the teams a measure of success beyond money

...and Accountability

  • Salary
  • + portion of profit from product (if profitable)
  • + portion of proceeds from spin-off
